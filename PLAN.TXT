Each Grade needs:
- A unique ID (so we can identify it)
- The grade score (what to do)
- Subject (what category it is in)
- Quarter (extra detail for the grade tracker)

So our data structure looks like:

{
  "id": 1,
  "score": 90,
  "subject": english,
  "quarter": "first"
}
